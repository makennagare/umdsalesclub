About Us

The Sales Club is a club designed to give students of any major a leg up on gaining real-world knowledge and career opportunities in sales. Students participating in Sales Club can expect to have opportunities at their disposal such as sales competitions, networking events, leadership opportunities, interview preparation, and community involvement. Our mission statement is to connect students to sales careers.

Whether you're a seasoned sales pro or just starting to explore the world of sales, our club offers something for everyone. Join us to build your skills, expand your network, and take your career to the next level!

Mentorship Program & Alumni Highlights

Sales Club Mentorship Program

Bridging the experience gap through professional connections.

The Sales Club Mentorship Program fosters meaningful personal and professional connections between underclassmen and upperclassmen while building stronger bonds between new and veteran members. The goal is to inspire ongoing professional growth.

  • Career development strategies
  • Industry networking opportunities
  • Interview preparation and feedback

Whether you're looking for guidance on career paths, interview prep, or just want to connect with someone who's been in your shoes, our mentorship program is here to support you every step of the way.
